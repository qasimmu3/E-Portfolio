Module 10 | Reflective Piece (Resubmission)

Introduction

Starting this module greatly impacted my academic path through constant feedback and opportunities to improve my research skills. The feedback I received suggesting areas of improvement has further motivated me to carry out research with renewed zeal and to provide a thorough examination of the primary topic. At the start of this module, I had several areas that needed polishing up, and each offered a chance for development and education. These insights strengthened my resolve to overcome challenges and gain a deeper understanding of the module.

 

Analysis and Interpretation

This module has highlighted vital points where I exceeded expectations and gaps. The feedback I received stressed that more focus needed to be applied. Since my work lacked a strong storyline or critique, the feedback noted that I put in more emphasis. Notably, my analysis went too deep into accounts of description instead of critically evaluating the research, significantly reducing the thoroughness and quality of such an examination. In addition, the feedback suggested I include more relevant sources to improve the strength of the literature review. With this in mind, I plan to enhance my research approach by being more critical and analytic while incorporating many perspectives to write a complete analysis in the final section of this work.

Additionally, part of the feedback noted a commendable deep knowledge of the subject and well-referenced significant points. Nevertheless, the work failed to outline the importance of the results and how they related more broadly to the scope of this research. Moving forward, I will capitalize on my strengths while addressing weaknesses such as analytic thinking and communication skills.

Furthermore, the feedback suggested having a more structured and systematic approach to sourcing and citing appropriate literature. They stressed sticking to academic norms and correct referencing. Thus, I need to focus on details and precision in academic writing to cover all the elements of the research. The feedback also mentioned the importance of a coherent flow to understand the research objectives and findings. Hence, based on these perspectives, I plan to improve my research methodology and academic writing skills. 

 

Statistical Analysis Skills

                Apart from analyzing and interpreting data, the statistical portion of the module has improved my research abilities. I have gained a deeper understanding of crucial measurable tests through hypothesis testing with Excel. The Related Samples T Test allowed me to evaluate the significance of contrasts between related groups, whereas the Independent Samples T Test permitted me to compare autonomous groups. Additionally, I honed my skills in applying the Two-Tailed Test, which analyzes both sides of a hypothesis. The task introduced me to making Bar Charts, allowing effective information visualization. I  ensure to apply these statistical skills towards my final project, while still building on my existing knowledge.

 

Research Methods Process based on my Learning

My research aims to investigate the influence of information systems (IS) on the healthcare segment within the United States. My approach includes a profound understanding of critical standards and strategies established within the scientific strategy, which support research in different disciplines. Throughout this course, I have examined different research methodologies and plans, fitting them to particular needs. My thirst for knowledge also includes the improvement of essential research competencies such as collecting and reviewing differing data sorts, allowing for a critical evaluation of independent research. I aim to contribute to a broader understanding of the subject by assessing existing literature and learning research techniques.

This module has also allowed me a reflective and autonomous approach to learning, regularly improving the quality and depth of my research. I have also appreciated the professional, legal, social, cultural, and ethical measurements that affect computing professionals, particularly in healthcare. This research involves the standards of academic examination and applying them to the dynamic field of healthcare IS. It fundamentally assesses the existing literature, research strategies, and data analysis forms, which are fundamental for a comprehensive understanding of the subject.

 

Action Plan

Strategies for Enhancing Personal and Professional Development

I plan to regularly undertake critical appraisal exercises, enabling me to understand the research exercise better. I intend to expand my knowledge by studying various academic sources and developing a well-rehearsed research perspective that acknowledges multiple viewpoints. Further, I will attend seminars that will equip me with proficiency in explaining complicated concepts well and attractively.

Plan for Implementing Suggested Improvements

To achieve my plan, I shall revisit aspects such as critical analysis, explore various empirical approaches such as quantitative and qualitative methods, and conduct exercise-based practical cases relating to my study. I also plan to cultivate an organized referencing manner that strictly follows academic standards. These practices will allow me to have an improved, sounder research process for better and more significant literary texts in the future.

 

Personal Growth

 

Reflection on the Impact of Module Failure

Failing in this module taught me the importance of resilience and determination when facing academic obstacles. I now consider failure an opportunity to develop physically and emotionally into a resilient individual. Thus, I have decided to change how I view academics to accept problems as an inevitable part of any educational process. Adaptability, adequate time for managing the equilibrium between responsibilities at work, and education help fulfill personal and professional targets. It has also emphasized the importance of proactive planning and selective prioritizing to allocate time in different areas of responsibility effectively. Additionally, experience in this module has highlighted the importance of being always ready to adjust to the rapidly changing business world.

 

Conclusion

This module has been a significant turning point toward my personal and career growth. From the feedback, I have gained essential advice about being resilient, adapting well, and managing my available time appropriately in academia and at the workplace. Understanding these challenges has helped deepen my knowledge of what is required to make good quality and impactful scholarly contributions. In the future, I yearn to put into practice all the lessons learned and feedback while preparing for the final Cyber Security project. As I look forward to my academic future, I will take this knowledge and make a difference in cyber security as a professional.
